Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

SmartDraft AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. What We Collect

  • We may store non-personal preferences such as your selected writing tone or persona, license key, and usage quota using Chrome local storage.
  • We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., feature usage counts) to improve the product experience.
  • If you activate a license key, we store basic usage and plan info on our secure server.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect your email, contacts, messages, or passwords.
  • We do not access or store personal communication content.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

3. Host Permissions & Site Access

SmartDraft AI requires access to certain websites (like Gmail, LinkedIn, WhatsApp Web) only to provide contextual rewriting inside compose boxes. We do not monitor browsing history or track user activity outside of supported platforms.

4. Remote Server Usage

SmartDraft AI uses a secure backend API to validate license keys, enforce usage quotas, and store anonymous analytics. No executable code is fetched or executed remotely.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at smartdraft.ai@gmail.com.

This policy may be updated as the product evolves. Changes will be reflected on this page.
